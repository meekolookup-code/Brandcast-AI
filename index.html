What we check

Every pixel, perfectly on-brand

Comprehensive brand compliance checks across every dimension that matters — automatically, every time.

Color Compliance
Detects off-brand colors, missing brand palette usage, and contrast issues. Compared pixel-by-pixel to your approved palette.
Typography Audit
Scans for unauthorized fonts, incorrect weights, and type hierarchy violations against your brand's typographic rules.
Logo Usage
Checks clear space rules, minimum size requirements, unauthorized modifications, and incorrect logo placement.
Platform Dimensions
Validates that assets meet exact pixel requirements for every major social media platform, display ad standard, and print spec.
Guideline Parsing
Upload your brand guidelines as PDF, PPTX, or DOCX. Our AI extracts and applies rules automatically — no manual setup.
Compliance Score
Every asset receives a detailed 0–10 compliance score with category breakdowns, actionable violation notes, and revision history.
